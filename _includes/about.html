About My Changes

First off, I changed the color of the text throughout. I found the light gray to be a bit easier on the eyes.

Second, I added a couple different elements for displaying content: Horizontal Elements, and Vertical Elements.

This version of grayscale is brought to you by Anthony Leonardi and it's based on the Jekyll version by Jerome Lachaud

This theme features stock photos by Gratisography along with a custom Google Maps skin courtesy of Snazzy Maps.

Grayscale includes full HTML, CSS, and custom JavaScript files along with LESS files for easy customization.
